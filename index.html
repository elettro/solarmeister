Brand basics

Fast reference for the Shopify build, approvals, and production handoff.

Usage rules

  • Use horizontal logos for Shopify header and email.
  • Use square logos for profile tiles and app-style placements.
  • Use vertical logos only where space is narrow.
  • Do not move the 3-panel mark away from the M.

Brand voice

German B2C solar ecommerce. Clear, helpful, practical. Lead with easy setup, savings, product fit, and trust. Avoid heavy technical language on storefront surfaces.
